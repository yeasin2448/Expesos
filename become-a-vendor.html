    FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

    Here are some common questions about selling on Xpesos

    How do fees work on Xpesos?

    Joining and starting a shop on Xpesos is free. Product Listing is free, you only pay when you sell.

    It does not cost you anything to publish a listing to the marketplace. A listing lasts for four months or until the item is sold. Once an item sells, there is a 0% transaction fee on the sale price (including shipping costs). If you accept payments with PayPal, there may be payment processing fee based on their fee structure.

    How do I get paid?

    If you accept payments with PayPal, funds from PayPal sales on Xpesos will be deposited into your PayPal account after a completed transaction. Not necessary but we encourage sellers to use a PayPal Business account and not a Personal account, as personal accounts may be subjected to monthly receiving limits .

    What do I need to do to create a shop?

    It’s easy to set up a shop on Xpesos. Create an Xpesos account (if you don’t already have one), set your shop location and currency, choose a shop name, create a listing, set a payment method (how you want to be paid), and finally set a billing method (how you want to pay your Xpesos fees.

    Do I need a credit or debit card to create a shop?

    No, a credit or debit card is not required to create a shop. To be verified as a seller you have the choice to use either a driver licence or to register via Facebook.
